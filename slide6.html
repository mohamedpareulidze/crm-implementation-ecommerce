Definition
In this project, a CRM is not a “sales tool” — it is a platform that centralizes customer data (profiles, purchase history, communication records) and captures behavioral data to enable personalized engagement across the customer lifecycle.
Behavior tracking
Track interactions consistently (touchpoints, browsing, purchases) to understand customer behavior and preferences.
Automation
Use CRM-driven flows to improve engagement and performance (recovery, lifecycle messages, targeted campaigns).
Customer lifecycle
Coordinate marketing and service actions across lifecycle stages, not isolated one-time interactions.
