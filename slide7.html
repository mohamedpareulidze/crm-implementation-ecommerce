Checklist — what the CRM must deliver
Events visit • purchase • abandoned cart • support
Customer data
Centralize profiles, orders and interaction history to build a unified customer view.
Behavior events
Track key events consistently (visits, purchases, abandoned carts) to understand behavior and journey.
Automation
Run segmentation and automated email/SMS flows to make engagement personalized and scalable.
Integration
Integrate smoothly with the e-commerce platform and existing tools to avoid manual exports and duplicated data.
Ease of use
Fast adoption by non-technical users (marketing + customer support) with minimal training burden.
Takeaway: The selected CRM must excel at behavior-based engagement (events + automation) while staying easy to integrate and easy to use.
